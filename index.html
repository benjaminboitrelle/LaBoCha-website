About LaBoCha

LaBoCha is an innovative folk-jazz trio based in Hamburg, Germany, blending chanson, jazz, gypsy rhythms, and subtle oriental influences. Our music combines poetic storytelling with intricate melodies, creating a sound that feels both intimate and expansive.

Led by French songwriter and composer Giohm, we weave together French folk traditions, jazz improvisation, and Eastern musical textures to create something unique and timeless.
